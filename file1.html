Align Content

Usage:

align-content: stretch | center | flex-start | flex-end | space-between | space-around | initial | inherit;

This property modifies the VERTICAL spacing behavior of content on the page.

Note: this property does NOT work on Internet Explorer (version 10 and below) or on Safari (version 6 and below)!

Example:

align-content: stretch center flex-start flex-end space-between space-around initial

Align Items

Usage:

align-items: stretch | center | flex-start | flex-end | baseline | initial | inherit;

This property modifies the VERICAL spacing behavior of items on the page.

Note: this property does NOT work on Internet Explorer (version 10 and below) or on Safari (version 6 and below)!

Example:

align-items: stretch center flex-start flex-end baseline initial

Align Self

Usage:

align-self: auto | stretch | center | flex-start | flex-end | baseline | initial | inherit;

This property modifies the VERTICAL spacing behavior of a specific item on the page.

Note: this property does NOT work on Internet Explorer (version 10 and below) or on Safari (version 6 and below)!

Note: this property will override the container's align-items property!

Example:

align-self: auto stretch center flex-start flex-end baseline initial

Justify Content

Usage:

justify-content: center | flex-start | flex-end | space-around | space-between | initial | inherit;

This property modifies the HORIZONTAL spacing behavior of content on the page.

Note: this property does NOT work on Internet Explorer (version 10 and below) or on Safari (version 6 and below)!

Example:

justify-content: center flex-start flex-end space-around space-between initial

Margin

Usage:

margin: length | auto | inherit | initial;

This property sets the spacing OUTSIDE the element.

Providing [w] as the length will set w for margin on all sides.

Providing [w x] as length will set w for top and bottom margins and x for left and right margins.

Providing [w x y] as length will set w for top, x for left AND right, and y for bottom margins.

Providing [w x y z] as length will set w for top, x for right, y for bottom, and z for left margins.

Example:

This example is setting the margin of the green box

margin: px [all sides]

Text Align Last

Usage:

text-align-last: auto | left | right | center | justify | start | end | initial | inherit;

This property sets the alignment of the last line of a paragraph

Note: This property is not supported in Safari

Note: For use in Firefox, include the -moz- vendor prefix

Note: Internet Explorer does not support the start or end options

Example:

justify-content: auto left right center justify start end initial
